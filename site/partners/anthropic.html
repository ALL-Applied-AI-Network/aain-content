02

Why Anthropic, specifically

The student AI org space is consolidating. OpenAI just announced its ChatGPT Futures program for Fall 2026 — a top- down, school-by-school student leader program. We respect the move, and we think there’s a better answer: a network owned by the students themselves, with Claude as the working AI tool inside it.

Anthropic already operates several education programs in parallel: Claude for Education campus deals (Northeastern, LSE, Champlain), Campus Ambassadors, Claude Builder Clubs, the CodePath partnership, the Teach For All partnership. What you don’t have is a single chapter network that ties all of those together — shared playbooks, a unified dashboard, engagement metrics across schools, and an industry sponsor pipeline. That’s what we are.

The deal we’re proposing is small for you and large for us:

  • You provide: Claude API credits and Claude Code access for chapter eboards and members — the same package Campus Ambassadors already get, just routed through one network instead of N negotiations.
  • We provide: default Claude integration in chapter onboarding, the dashboard, and the curriculum. New chapters inherit it. Engagement metrics ship back to you so the partnership is measurable.
  • We co-build: a Claude-specific learning path in the open-source Learning Tree (“Build your first MCP server,” “Ship a Claude-powered chapter project,” etc.). Free curriculum for any university to adopt.
  • You get: a public, dated metric you can point at. “X% of network chapters using Claude. Y projects shipped this quarter. Z hires from chapters where Claude was the working tool.”

04

What we’re NOT going to claim

We’re a few months in on the new platform. The MAIC chapter that seeded ALL has been running for four years and has 500+ members, but we haven’t yet onboarded the next 10. Other student-AI orgs we’ve been in conversation with are at varying stages of commitment. Anyone who quotes “30 chapters across the country” to you is rounding up — we won’t.

What we have is the playbook (proven over four years at MSOE), the software (chapter dashboard + sponsor portal + open-source content), the partner pipeline (NVIDIA active, Oliphant active, Anthropic in conversation, others queued), and a founder who has done this once and is doing it again at scale. We’d rather be honest about that than pitch you on a number we can’t back up.
