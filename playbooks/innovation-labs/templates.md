# Templates and Resources

This page contains ready-to-use templates for every stage of an Innovation Lab. Copy, adapt, and make them your own.

## 1. Sponsor Pitch Deck Outline

Use this structure for a 10-slide pitch deck when approaching potential sponsors.

```
Slide 1: Title
  [Club Name] Innovation Lab Partnership
  [University Name] | [Semester Year]

Slide 2: Who We Are
  - Club name, founding date, member count
  - One-line mission statement
  - 2-3 highlight stats (events run, members engaged, past sponsors)

Slide 3: What Is an Innovation Lab?
  - Multi-week structured AI competition
  - Student teams solve a real industry problem
  - Supported by sponsor mentors and data

Slide 4: What We Need From You
  - A business problem with associated data
  - 2-3 technical mentors (10-15 hours total)
  - Sponsorship funding (reference your tier structure)

Slide 5: What You Get
  - Direct access to [X] motivated AI/ML students
  - 4-8 teams producing solutions to your problem
  - Final presentations with code and recommendations
  - First look at top talent for recruiting

Slide 6: Timeline
  - Visual 5-week timeline with key milestones

Slide 7: Past Results (if available)
  - Brief case study from a previous Lab
  - Or: example problems from similar programs

Slide 8: Sponsorship Tiers
  - Table of tiers with pricing and benefits

Slide 9: Testimonials (if available)
  - Student quote about the experience
  - Past sponsor quote about the value

Slide 10: Next Steps
  - Suggest a 20-minute scoping call
  - Your contact information
```

## 5. Timeline Template

Adapt dates for your semester.

```markdown
# Innovation Lab Timeline - [Semester Year]

## Pre-Launch (2-3 weeks before kickoff)
- [ ] Sponsor agreement signed
- [ ] Problem brief finalized and approved by sponsor
- [ ] Data transfer completed and verified
- [ ] Sign-up form open, promoted to members
- [ ] Room booked for kickoff and showcase
- [ ] Communication channels created

## Week 1: Kickoff
- [ ] Monday: Kickoff event (2 hours)
- [ ] Wednesday: Confirm all teams have data access
- [ ] Friday: Milestone 1 due (problem statement, EDA, candidate approaches)

## Week 2: Building
- [ ] Monday: Milestone 1 feedback distributed
- [ ] Tue/Thu: Sponsor office hours (2 sessions)
- [ ] Friday: Milestone 2 due (working baseline, metrics, pipeline)

## Week 3: Iteration
- [ ] Monday: Milestone 2 feedback distributed
- [ ] Tuesday: Cross-team standup (15 min each)
- [ ] Tue/Thu: Sponsor office hours (2 sessions)
- [ ] Friday: Milestone 3 due (improved solution, error analysis)

## Week 4: Polish
- [ ] Monday: Slide template and presentation tips shared
- [ ] Wednesday: Dry run presentations (team-to-team feedback)
- [ ] Thursday: Final slides submitted
- [ ] Friday: Milestone 4 due (final code, written summary)

## Week 5: Showcase
- [ ] Monday-Wednesday: Final practice and prep
- [ ] Thursday or Friday: Showcase event (2.5 hours)
- [ ] Same day: Thank-you messages sent

## Post-Lab (1-2 weeks after)
- [ ] Student feedback survey sent (within 48 hours)
- [ ] Sponsor debrief meeting (within 1 week)
- [ ] Debrief report sent to sponsor
- [ ] Results published on website and social media
- [ ] Judge feedback distributed to teams
```

## 6. Communication Templates

### Kickoff Reminder (send 3 days before)

```
Subject: Innovation Lab Kickoff - [Date] at [Time]

Hi everyone,

The Innovation Lab kicks off this [day] at [time] in [room].
Here is what you need to know:

- Bring your laptop (charged)
- Teams will be announced at the event
- You will get data access and start exploring on day one
- Duration: 2 hours

If you signed up and cannot make it, let us know ASAP at [email].
See you there.

[Your name]
```

### Milestone Reminder (send 24 hours before deadline)

```
Subject: Milestone [X] Due Tomorrow

Teams,

Milestone [X] is due tomorrow ([date]) by [time].

Deliverables:
- [List from the milestone description]

Submit via [method: shared drive link, email, form].

Questions? Post in #innovation-lab-questions or come to
office hours today at [time].

[Your name]
```

### Post-Showcase Thank You

```
Subject: Thank You - Innovation Lab [Semester Year]

Hi everyone,

Thank you for an incredible Innovation Lab. Here are the highlights:

- [X] teams competed over [X] weeks
- Winner: [Team name] with [brief description]
- [Any other notable results]

Special thanks to [Sponsor] for providing the problem, data,
and mentorship that made this possible.

Photos: [link]
Results: [link]

If you have not filled out the feedback survey yet, please
do so by [date]: [link]

We are already planning next semester. Stay tuned.

[Your name]
```
